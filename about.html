About Me

I'm a Web Developer from Monroe, Washington

Web Development is a fun, challenging, and exciting field to be in, and I have loved every minute of it. It is fun to be apart of a great team, building something that is both beautiful and functional. It is always my goal to help create something that strikes this delicate balance. However, it can be a challenge to find that ballance between function and beauty. But coming across these challenges is something that I love. It allows me to dive deep into the code, learn something new, and find a creative solution to solve the challenges that come up in the process. That's where I get excited, finding a solution to the problem by learning something new, and applying it.

If you don't see me typing away on my computer, then you are likely to find me exercising, hiking, cooking, or just spending time with family and friends.
